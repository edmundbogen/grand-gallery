# The Grand Gallery 🖼️

A first-person, walkable 3D art museum in your browser. Stroll through a marble
hall, walk up to ten of the world's most famous paintings, and click any one to
read its story, history, and notable details — plus zoom into close-up details.

**▶ Walk through it live:** https://edmundbogen.github.io/grand-gallery/

## Controls
- **W A S D** / arrow keys — move
- **Mouse** — look around
- **Click a painting** (when the crosshair turns gold) — open its info panel
- **Close-up tiles** in the panel — click to zoom into a detail
- **Esc** — release the cursor

## The Collection
Mona Lisa · The Starry Night · The Scream · Girl with a Pearl Earring ·
The Birth of Venus · The Great Wave off Kanagawa · The Kiss · Water Lilies ·
The Night Watch · Las Meninas.

All artwork is in the public domain. Built with [Three.js](https://threejs.org).
